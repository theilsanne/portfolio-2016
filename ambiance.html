Brief

Campuses need to encourage more social media interaction, promotion, and aura around its campus life.

Ambi치nce provides a way to visualise the social media coverage of events around campus through a mobile application and interactive visualisation table, using geo-location and hashtag data taken from social media.


Understanding

A large part of the current issue with the lack of social media presence is due to the lack of centralisation of social media content across platforms.

Having conducted a questionnaire to gauge the campus' social media impact on its students, I found that event awareness is limited despite users following some form of student life/campus life account. With this in mind, I proposed the following features:

Mobile application
  • Augmented reality camera to visualise aura in person
  • Ability to synchronise social media platforms
  • Social media feed built from source hashtag/geolocation data
  • Real time updating of visualisation
  • History of campus aura (daily, weekly, monthly)
Installation
  • AView large and/or trending eventsn
  • History of campus aura
  • Shows an overview of the aura

Wireframes

Visual Design

I chose a vibrant and loud mood that complements abstract and organic forms. As an application that visualises campus aura, it only makes sense to have a vibrant scheme.

Preliminary Design

The first round of compositions followed the wireframes very closely, and was too conservative and clean for the vibrant and exciting mood that the application was meant to be.

The first interactive installation was heavily based off of the map screen, and was focused on the social media aspect - showing real time posts.

Final Compositions

Much more vibrant and visually interesting in style, the final composition lives up to the hype that the application promises to bring with carefully placed graphical elements and attention-catching visualisations of campus event aura.


Motion Prototypes

Motion prototypes that illustrate the interactions of both the application and the installation.



Fin

Ambi치nce brings campus events into the spotlight by encouraging social media posts and interaction.

By providing aura visualisations as a platform for users that are both seeking out events and promoting events, Ambi치nce is a great tool for seeing event-related content on social media, all in the same place, serving as the bridge between social media content aggregation and event promotion.

This project was very tough, as there was a time crunch but I was ambitious regardless to complete many assets for the presentation. In doing so I truly understood the amount of time and effort it takes to pull together a project presentation that I myself would be proud of showing, within a short time frame.
